GigBuddy — Mood Boards

Three distinct directions for the look and feel. Each commits to a different opinion about what GigBuddy is. Scroll through, react, then pick one (or remix).

Performance cards are rendered at iPhone 13 width (390pt) to give a realistic sense of the live view. Test on your actual phone in a dim room before committing.
